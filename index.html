About

I am a computer scientist and artificial intelligence researcher.
My research studies the underlying computational principles of intelligent decision making with a focus on reinforcement learning. In my work I find applications of reinforcement learning, machine learning, and deep learning models and algorithms in cognitive science, neuroscience, and other disciplines.
Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Mila - Quebec AI Institute. In spring 2021 I received my PhD in Computer Science from Brown University.
