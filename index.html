Lucas Lehnert

PhD Student in Computer Science

lucas_lehnert at brown dot edu  

Since Fall 2016 I am a PhD student at Brown University advised by Michael Littman. Before joining Brown University I obtained a Master's and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from McGill University under the supervision of Doina Precup. My resume can be found here.

I am interested in developing algorithms that can learn to solve a wide variety of problems without having to adopt the algorithm to the task at hand. Hence most of my work falls into the category of Reinforcement Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. During my undergraduate studies I also worked on projects in computer vision, signal processing, and robotics.
